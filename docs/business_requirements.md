# Business Requirements

## Objective

Create a management reporting solution for payment operations, finance,
reconciliation, risk, and data quality.

## Stakeholders

- Finance
- Payment Operations
- Risk and Compliance
- Product
- Executive Management

## Core requirements

1. Track transaction count and value.
2. Measure success, failure, reversal, and pending rates.
3. Monitor processing times and service-level breaches.
4. Reconcile completed transactions to settlement records.
5. Identify missing settlements and amount mismatches.
6. Monitor suspicious transaction indicators.
7. Report data-quality issues.
8. Provide drill-down views by channel and province.
